Buddha Bar

This site was built as a stock management system for online sales. Our primary focuse is on small, independent sellers. The site pulls data in from authenticated sites and polls for changes. If an item is sold on one site it is removed from the others. Users are also able to update stock on all sites via the dash board. Node, Angular, Mongoose, MongoDB, Express, OAuth and various API's
